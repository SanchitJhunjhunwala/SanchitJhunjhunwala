Sanchit Jhunjhunwala

I'm a student. And I intend to keep it that way.

Overarching a range of experiences - academic, managerial and entrepreneurial - my time as an upskilling member of the human race has been enriched by the learnings brought forth with each insight. Whether it was tinkering, training or team building; each train of thought has been originated and culminated in the same spirit - to identify, define and solve problems.

Education

Schooling my way through numerous towns and cities across India, I've been rather fortunate in terms of the early exposure I've had to diversity - of problems and ideas. Amid all this change, what I did find as a constant was my love for all things mechanical. And this has since driven my examination of things, concepts and that of understanding itself.

Experience

Projects
